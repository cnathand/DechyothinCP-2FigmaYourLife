Gaming

I’ve been playing video games since I was a kid, and they’ve always been a big part of my life. I enjoy all kinds of games, from the good ones to even the not so great ones. I prefer multiplayer games, especially when I can play with friends, over single-player experiences. For me, gaming has always been a casual hobby something I do to relax and unwind, never something I take too seriously. I used to play a lot of MMORPGs but now I have no time for that as it is very time consuming. Now I play games with quick matches like Fortnite, Valorant, or League of Legends.
